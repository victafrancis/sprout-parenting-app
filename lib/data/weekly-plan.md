## üß∫ Weekly Activity Menu

Pick 3‚Äì5 per day, repeat favorites. These are short (2‚Äì8 minutes), flexible options ‚Äî not a rigid schedule.

### Monday

**1. Drop & Watch (Trajectory)**
- Materials: Laundry basket + 3 soft balls
- Adult prompt: "Ready‚Ä¶ drop!" (pause, wait for eye contact)
- Extend: Move basket farther away

**2. Crawl Chase (Gross Motor)**
- Roll ball slowly across room
- Encourage retrieval
- Celebrate effort, not speed

**3. Peek-a-Boo Cloth (Enveloping)**
- Hide toy under cloth
- Wait for child to pull it away

### Tuesday

**1. Towel Ramp Roll**
- Fold towel to create slight ramp
- Roll ball down
- Encourage crawling after it

**2. Container Fill & Dump**
- Large blocks + big bowl
- Practice "in‚Ä¶ out!"

**3. Mealtime Language Pause**
- When child drops spoon, say: "You dropped it." (pause)
- Wait for response before picking up

### Wednesday

**1. Water Play (Supervised)**
- Small bowl + cup
- Drip water from cloth into bowl
- Label: "Drip‚Ä¶ splash."

**2. Cushion Crawl Path**
- Couch cushions on floor
- Encourage crawling over soft obstacles

**3. Diapering Participation (Routine Focus)**

Script:
- "I'm going to change your diaper." (pause)
- "Can you lift your bottom?" (wait)
- Allow child to drop old diaper into bin.

### Thursday

**1. Rolling Under Table (Orientation + Trajectory)**
- Roll ball so it goes partially under chair
- Encourage problem-solving retrieval

**2. Sock Transfer (Transporting Bridge Skill)**
- Move socks from laundry basket to box

**3. Mirror Babble Game**
- Copy child's sounds
- Pause and wait
- Encourage back-and-forth exchange

### Friday

**1. Drop into Different Containers**
- Basket vs. bowl vs. box
- Observe if child adjusts hand movement

**2. Hide & Find Toy**
- Partially cover toy with cloth
- Wait before assisting

**3. Mealtime Choice**
- Offer: "Water or milk?"
- Pause for gesture or sound.

### Weekend (Repeat & Extend)

Repeat the activities your child engaged with most. Repetition strengthens learning at this age.
